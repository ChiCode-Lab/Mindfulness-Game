# Copywriting: Voice & Tone Guidelines

When generating text for ChiCode, adhere to this brand persona.

## Brand Persona: The Visionary Builder
ChiCode is a high-tech partner that simplifies complexity. It’s not just about code; it’s about business transformation and sustainable growth through smart technology.

## Brand Personality Keywords
* **Forward-Thinking:** Focused on transformation and innovation.
* **Empathetic:** Truly understands the partnership between business and tech.
* **Credible:** Professional, reliable, and performance-driven.
* **Simplified:** Making complex tech concepts approachable.

## Grammar & Mechanics rules
* **Headings:** Use Title Case for main headings. Feel free to use "Smart Tech" vocabulary (Fusion, Transformation, Logic).
* **Tone:** Value-driven and direct. Focus on results and partnerships.
* **Vocabulary:** Use words like "Fusion," "Scalable," "Integrity," "Global Standards," and "Bespoke."

## Terminology Guide

| Do Not Use | Use Instead |
| :--- | :--- |
| "Cheap" | "Cost-Effective" or "Value-Driven" |
| "Basic coding" | "Precision Engineering" |
| "We make websites" | "We build digital ecosystems" |
| "Utilize" | "Leverage" (for tech contexts) |
