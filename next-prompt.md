A 3-step onboarding flow introduction to the core shape-identification mechanic of our gamified mindfulness application. This screen specifically represents the starting step "Welcome / Step 1".

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Mobile-first
- Theme: Dark, calming, minimal, ethereal
- Background: Deep Twilight Blue (#1A233A)
- Surface: Soft Midnight (#222E4A) for modal cards
- Primary Accent: Soft Coral (#FF8A65) for the subtle visual changes and CTA buttons
- Text Primary: Off-White (#F8F9FA) for high readability without strain
- Text Secondary: Muted Lavender (#B0BEC5) for secondary labels and hints
- Grid Elements: Smooth, rounded shapes (fully rounded pills, soft circles, organic blobs)
- Cards/Popups: Gently rounded (16px), very soft, diffused shadow

**Page Structure:**
1. **Header:** Extremely minimal top right header offering a "Skip" option in Muted Lavender.
2. **Visual Area (Center top):** A large, ethereal glowing soft coral shape that is gently floating on the deep twilight blue, shifting slightly to demonstrate the visual changes players must identify. Use soft glassmorphism blur beneath the shape.
3. **Information Area (Center bottom):** 
    - Heading: "Find the Subtlety" in Off-White.
    - Paragraph text: "Take a deep breath. Focus your eyes on the grid and identify the shape that is slightly different from the rest." in Muted Lavender.
4. **Action Area (Bottom):** 
    - Soft Coral primary CTA: "Next".
    - Progress indicators: Three small, glowing dots above the button (e.g., the first one active in soft coral, the other two muted lavender).
